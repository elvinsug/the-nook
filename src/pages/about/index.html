Our Story

Why We Built This For Students

I was that student pulling all-nighters in Starbucks and libraries, spending $30/day, fighting for a seat with a power socket. There had to be a better way.

So I created The Nook - a space where students can study affordably, guilt-free, with guaranteed seats and every amenity you need to succeed.

~ Elvin Sugianto, Founder of The Nook (Final Year Student at NTU)

Our Journey

From Idea to Reality

Every great space starts with a simple problem. Here's how we turned student struggles into solutions.

Late 2023

The Problem Identified

As a university student, I found myself spending $30+ daily at cafes, fighting for seats with power outlets, and feeling guilty about staying too long. Something had to change.

Early 2024

The Vision Takes Shape

We surveyed 500+ students and discovered everyone faced the same struggle. The solution? A dedicated student space with affordable food, guaranteed seats, and zero pressure to leave.

Mid 2024

Building The Nook

Months of planning, designing, and partnering with local suppliers who believed in our mission. Every detail was crafted with students in mind—from desk heights to lighting.

October 2024

Grand Opening

We opened our doors to the student community. The response was overwhelming—students finally had a space they could call their own, without breaking the bank.

Today & Beyond

Growing Together

We're constantly evolving based on your feedback. Better amenities, and a stronger community—because your success is our mission.
